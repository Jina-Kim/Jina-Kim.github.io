About

I am a M. Sc. student in Department of Interaction Science at Sungkyunkwan University and a member of Data eXperience Lab advised by Eunil Park.
My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and human-computer interaction. My research objective is to solve real-world problems by finding valuable cues in online services or social media with the support of user-oriented machine learning models.
