About

I am a M. Sc. student in the Department of Interaction Science at Sungkyunkwan University and a member of Data eXperience Lab advised by Prof. Eunil Park. My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and human-computer interaction. I am highly interested in addressing societal problems with human-centered machine learning, where I mainly focus on capturing valuable cues in online services or social media.
