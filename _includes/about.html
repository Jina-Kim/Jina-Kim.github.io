About

I am a M. Sc. student in the Department of Interaction Science at Sungkyunkwan University and a member of Data eXperience Lab advised by Prof. Eunil Park. My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and human-computer interaction. Currently, I mainly focus on designing and building machine learning models from human-centered perspectives, which reflect human needs and further address societal problems. Moreover, I attempt to validate human-centered machine learning models by conducting social science methodologies beyond accuracy. For more information about my research experience, please refer to my Curriculum Vitae (PDF).
